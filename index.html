Our mission for Education That INSPIRES is to create an organization for families to utilize from home that will help educate and inspire all children in pre-k and elementary school. We hope that this resource will make early childhood learning accessible and equitable for all children from the ages of 2 to 10 years old and will inspire and set all children up to achieve future success in their education. Our most important mission is to install a love of learning in children, that we hope they will carry with them into adolescence and adulthood.
